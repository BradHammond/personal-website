Megapixel Ideas

Megapixel Ideas is my freelance web development company. I needed a website to showcase my abilities, portfolio and various offerings. The Megapixel Ideas website has gone through several changes.

At its creation, I used Megapixel Ideas to promote mobile applications that I have created. The first iteration of the website was a promotional page for mobile apps. Currently the Megapixel Ideas website is in its third iteration, primarily focusing on web development services.

Christ Explained

Christ Explained is a non-profit organization that writes about Christian-based topics and distributes free Bibles across the globe. I was tasked with creating a website that was simple to navigate and reader-centric.

I designed this website with reading in mind and it is easy to read content whether you are on a desktop or mobile device. Overall this was a very different project and had goals that would be similar to a web application rather than a straightforward marketing website.

Alpine Baptist Church

Alpine Baptist Church needed a new and updated website that would reflect their current mission, provide access to updated resources and that would encourage people to come in and visit.

I started with their old website's content as a basis for the new website but completely redesigned the new site from the ground up. This was a small simple project that was a lot of fun and very rewarding.

Luci

Luci is a startup company that is part social media platform, part daily deal service. Luci was in the process of developing a mobile application and needed a designer to work through different ideas, designs and prototypes.

I handled user experience research, user experience design, wire-framing, prototyping, illustration, logo design and user interface design. I was able to create a brand identity and application design that Luci's development team can use to launch a viable application.
